How It Works

Welcome to Flat Rate Home Selling at Excel Real Estate, where we offer two different listing services to fit the needs of sellers looking to list their property. Whether you're looking for a limited service listing or a full-service listing, we've got you covered!

Our limited service listing, priced at just $399, is perfect for sellers who want to take control of their property listing. As agents in South Carolina, we'll list your property on the MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, and other selling platforms, and have you as the preferred contact when another agent or buyer is interested in your home. You'll be responsible for supplying your own photos and description for your home and we will post them to the many selling platforms to give you the most exposure to obtain buyers!

If you're looking for a more comprehensive listing service, our full-service listing is priced at $1,999 and includes everything in the limited service listing, as well as additional features. We'll list your property on the MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, and other selling platforms, and we'll even supply a lock box for your door which allows agents to get into your home for showings. We'll also provide a for sale sign in your yard and write a listing description for you. We will take professional photos, and give you real estate advice on contracts received and help with negotiations when it comes to dealing with buyers and other agents.

At Flat Rate Home Selling, we're committed to providing you with the best possible listing services at affordable prices. Whether you're looking for a limited service listing or a full-service listing, we're here to help you every step of the way.
